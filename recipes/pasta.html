Pasta with tomato sauce

Description

Pasta with tomato sauce is a very popular dish because it is filling, easy to make, and uses inexpensive ingredients. It is also a very versatile dish that may contain many different ingredients depending on personal preference. This is my go-to version of this classic meal.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb of penne pasta
  • 1/3 of a jar of preferred tomato sauce
  • 1 orange bell pepper
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 tbsp of butter
  • 1 slice of mozzarella cheese
  • salt
  • fresh parsley optional garnish

Steps

  1. Boil approx 3 quarts of water in a pot and heat 1/2 tbsp of butter in small saucepan
  2. Add pasta and about 2 tsp of salt to boiling water and cook for approx 10 minutes
  3. While pasta is cooking, dice bell pepper and garlic and add to saucepan, lightly garnish with salt
  4. After pasta is done cooking, drain remaining water and set aside the pasta
  5. Add tomato sauce and slice of cheese to the pot, cook until bubbling or cheese fully melted
  6. Combine pasta, bell pepper, and garlic in the pot, mix thouroughly
  7. Plate and add fresh parsley to garnish if desired
