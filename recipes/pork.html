Pork chops>

Description

Pork chops are a relatively inexpensive and easy to cook cut of meat. You can enjoy this dish with many different sides and sauces. This recipe is for cooking the chops on the stove top, but you can prepare them using other methods as well.

Ingredients

  • 4 thin boneless pork chops
  • 1 cup of flour
  • salt, pepper, and chili powder
  • 1 1/2 tbsp of oil, 1/2 tbsp of butter

Steps

  1. Heat oil and butter in a pan
  2. Combine flour and approx 2 tsp of salt and pepper in a small bowl, mix
  3. Season both sides of pork chops with salt, pepper, and chili powder
  4. Dredge chops in flour mixture and add to pan, cook about 5 minutes on each side
  5. Remove from heat and serve, pair with a small salad or your favorite veg. Recommended sauce pairing : BBQ
