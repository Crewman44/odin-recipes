Mac & Cheese

Description

Macaroni and cheese is a comfort food for many. Again, there are many different versions to suit one's individual tastes. This is how I like to make my mac.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb of Shell, Farfalle, or Elbow pasta
  • 1 boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • 3 slices of bacon
  • 1/4 cup of flour
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • salt, pepper, and chili powder

Steps

  1. Boil approx 3 quarts of water in a pot, when boiling, salt and add pasta.
  2. Cook pasta for about 8 minutes or until al dente. Drain
  3. Cook bacon in a small non-stick pan
  4. After bacon is done, season chicken breast with salt, pepper, and chili powder and cook in bacon grease, about 8 minutes on each side
  5. Combine flour, butter, 1 tsp salt, and 1 tsp pepper in a pot to make a roux
  6. When bubbling, add milk. Stir continuously
  7. When bubbling, add cheddar. Continue stirring until cheese is fully melted
  8. Dice chicken and bacon and combine with pasta in cheese sauce. Mix thouroughly
  9. Serve and garnish with BBQ sauce if desired
